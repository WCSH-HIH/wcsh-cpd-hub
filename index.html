ABOUT WCSH CPD CENTER

The WCSH Continuing Professional Development (CPD) Center was founded in 2014 by JSI with a vision to enhance healthcare quality through continuous learning and professional excellence. Our center serves as a hub for training, skill development, and knowledge advancement for healthcare professionals. We are committed to fostering a culture of lifelong learning and evidence-based practice, ensuring that our healthcare workforce remains at the forefront of medical innovation and patient care excellence.
